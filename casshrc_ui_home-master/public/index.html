About us

Every year, millions of Students lose valuable "first" employment opportunities with Corporates around the nation. These opportunities are lost as there is no dedicated platform for such freshers to approach Corporates that don't conduct Campus Placement drive in their colleges. As a result, they opt for opportunities that are not in-sync with their career objective.

Every year, 1000s of Corporates (small/ medium/ large) lose upon millions of high potential candidates for opportunities within their organization due to costs involved and no guarantee of accuracy/ closures in the Campus Placement drive. As a result, Corporates spend a huge amount of money in closure of such positions, while going for the second best option.

Prime Careers brings a viable solution for such freshers and such corporates. We are a "freshers only" platform that enables where freshers and corporates to directly interact with each other for the initial campus placement drive, and finalize closures only when they are sure about taking the process ahead. We enable:

  1. Students to choose the right opportunities for a fresher amongst 1000s of opportunities available across the nation, at any place at any time at a very nominal cost
  2. Corporates to assess students available for Campus Placements drive in their own office from various states and complete up to 85% of the Campus Placement Drive, saving huge costs and plan commitments to business accordingly
